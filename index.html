About

Fitness has much more to do than just having a slimmer waist. It is defined by how you feel; it is a lifestyle that you follow for the value of your soundness rather than people's judgments. Through this program, I'll be guiding you to get back to your basics and form a sustainable plan that can be followed as a lifestyle. Because deprivation cannot be a way of life. This is a fitter way out from those boring fads that you have been hearing, as I am here to help you break the cycle of being on and off a diet. SAY YES TO YOUR FAVOURITE FOODS, BE IT THE NORTH INDIAN RAJMA CHAWAL OR PARATHAS. I am here to add balance over restriction.
